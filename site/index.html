Welcome to BookWyrm

Social Reading and Reviewing, Decentralized

BookWyrm is a social network for tracking your reading, talking about books, writing reviews, and discovering what to read next. Federation allows BookWyrm users to join small, trusted communities that can connect with one another, and with other ActivityPub services like Mastodon and Pleroma.

Track your reading

And what your friends are reading

Update your reading status as you start and finish books, post status updates as you go, and set an annual reading goal.

Discover books

Recommendations from people, not algorithms

See what your friends are reading, and get opinions from your community.

Share your thoughts

The way you want to

Write reviews or post casual commentary as you go, and control who gets to see your posts with granular privacy settings.

BookWyrm is collaborative, anti-corporate software maintained by Mouse Reeve.
